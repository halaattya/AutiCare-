AutiCare
A Mobile App for Children with Autism
AutiCare is my graduation project — a mobile app I built to support children with Autism Spectrum Disorder through interactive games, while giving parents the tools to actually understand and track their child's progress. It supports both Arabic and English, and was graded 97% at German Jordanian University.

Why I built this
I wanted to build something that actually matters. Children with autism need consistency, structure, and calm — and most apps out there either focus only on the child or only on the parent, not both. AutiCare tries to bridge that gap.

The part I'm most proud of — UI/UX
This is where I put the most thought and care. Designing for children with autism is not the same as designing for a general audience. I did a lot of research and consulted with a specialist before making design decisions.
Some of the choices I made on purpose:

Soft, muted colors at lower game levels to avoid sensory overload — colors get gradually more vivid as the child progresses
Large buttons, simple layouts, minimal choices on screen at any time
The child interface has zero access to settings, data, or anything that could be confusing or stressful
Everything is predictable — same layouts, same navigation, no surprises
Full Arabic RTL support without breaking any layouts
The parent dashboard is clean and structured so caregivers can find what they need fast

I genuinely believe that good design for accessibility is just good design. Every screen in this app was thought through, not just thrown together.

What the app does
For the child:

Level-based games targeting memory, attention, shapes, and emotion recognition
Calm Corner for emotional regulation — no scores, no pressure
Difficulty adapts automatically based on how the child is performing

For the parent:

PIN-protected parent space
Full child profile with developmental context
Weekly progress reports with per-game breakdowns
Daily log to record mood, sleep, triggers, behaviors
PDF export to share with therapists or teachers
Manual control over game levels and visibility
Multiple children supported under one account


Tech stack

Flutter + Dart
Firebase Authentication
Cloud Firestore
Android Studio
Client-side PDF generation
